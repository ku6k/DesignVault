# coreShapePalette
## Definition and Philosophy
The name coreShapePalette is a reflection of the fundamental role that shapes play in design and creativity. The term "core" signifies the essential and foundational nature of the shapes included in this library, emphasizing their importance in various design applications.

The word "Shape" highlights the versatility and adaptability of the forms, which can be transformed, combined, and manipulated to suit diverse creative needs.

Lastly, "Palette" suggests a collection of colors and tools available to artists and designers, allowing them to curate their unique visual expressions. Just as a painter uses a palette to blend colors and create new hues, designers can utilize the shapes in this library to craft innovative compositions and designs.

This name encapsulates the essence of creativity, inviting users to explore, experiment, and create with the fundamental building blocks of design. Whether for digital art, branding, or user interface design, coreShapePalette serves as a trusted resource for enhancing visual storytelling and artistic expression.
